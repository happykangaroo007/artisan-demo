Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from the Face Cream?
Colostrum Cream is our luxury, intensive treatment formula with caprine colostrum (not just goat milk) and exotic oils like marula and abyssinian. It's more concentrated, richer, and specifically targets advanced aging and scarring. Face Cream is our daily-use formula that's lighter and works for all skin types. Many customers use Colostrum Cream at night and Face Cream during the day.
Will this help with acne scars?
Yes! The growth factors in colostrum combined with evening primrose and sea buckthorn oil work together to fade hyperpigmentation and smooth scar tissue. Expect to see improvement within 6-8 weeks of consistent use. For active acne, we recommend our Face Cream instead.
Is this too rich for oily skin?
It is richer than our Face Cream, so if you have very oily skin, we recommend using it at night only or just on problem areas. The abyssinian oil actually helps balance oil production despite the rich texture. Many with combination skin love using this on dry areas and Face Cream on oilier zones.
What does it smell like?
Colostrum Cream is fragrance-free. You may detect a very subtle, natural scent from the sea buckthorn oil (slightly earthy/nutty), but it's not noticeable once applied and absorbed.
Can I use this during pregnancy?
Our ingredients are natural and gentle. However, we always recommend consulting your healthcare provider about any skincare products during pregnancy or nursing.
How long does a jar last?
Because this is a concentrated formula, a little goes a long way. Most customers find a 2 oz jar lasts 2-3 months with daily use on face and neck.
